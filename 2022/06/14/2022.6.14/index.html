# 6.13 写作大作文

Write about the following topic.
Many children these days have an unhealthy lifestyle. Both schools and parents are responsible for solving this problem.
To what extent do you agree with this statement?
Give reasons for your answer and include any relevant examples from your own knowledge or experience.

思考

  • Is it true that many children have an unhealthy lifestyle? Why?
  • Are parents responsible? Why? What should they do?
  • Are schools responsible? Why? What should they do?
  • Is anyone else responsible? Who?

Writing Plan

  • Para.1 - children have an unhealthy lifestyle,from many aspects,many factors,multi-dimension
  • Para.2 - parents responsible: be spoiled to,spare little time on children
  • Para.3 - schools responsible: peer competition,too much attention on score,little attention on mental health and physical health
  • Para.4 - social responsible: game industry regardless of children's self-discipline,overload education policy cause children cannot spare time on their daily life,takeout industry
  • Para.5 – conclusion: all of above are responsible for solving this problem.

Nowadays,many of the children suffer too much burden and temptation,which can lead them to form an unhealthy lifestyle like being late to sleep because of too much homework,being addicted to mobile games,too much reliance on takeout.These lifestyle comes with the development of the society from many aspects.Not only to the parents,but also the schools and society should be resiponsible for this problem.
As their guardian,some parents even didn't know what the grade their children is.They pay little attention to the growing process of the children.These part of children usually don't do well in their study and are addicted to computer games.They gradually get a poor eyesite due to spend too much time on the devices.Because of the temptation of games,they even forget to eat and drink,so they can't balance the life between work and rest.
In addition,most of the schools in our country are regardless of children's mental health and physical health,for making a high quality of competition environment to let more children get good score and go to acknowledged university,some of the teachers occupy the time for students to do exercises,this method leads some children ignore the significance of the sport exercise as time passes.
The other factor affecting children's irreglar lifestyle comes from the whole society.You may ask why the mobile games so popular among children in China.On the one hand,the government need the game industry to prosper the economy,on the other hand,the policy to these game companies have made restriction on the children less than 18 years old.However,no one can bear the temptation if he has been addicted to something.Besides,the policy on education has little concern about the diversity of the children.In fact,many unnecessary assignment affect children on their eating habit and rest habit,because it is impossible to have a completely consistent education policy.
In conclusion,the factors cause the forming of unhealthy lifestyle of children come from a wide range of sources,both the parents and the school should take some action to take children's physical and mental healthy into consideration.The education department needs to contact the whole society to pay attention to the cultivation of children's lifestyle.

Describe somewhere you like to shop for food.

You should say:

  • where this place is
  • what this place is like
  • what you buy there

and explain why you like buying food at this place.

Describe a meal that you enjoyed in a restaurant.

You should say:

  • where the restaurant was
  • what you ate
  • who ate the meal with you

and explain why you enjoyed eating the meal so much.
