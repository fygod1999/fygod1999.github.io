# 剑 12 Test 8 Section 2


The Sheepmarket is one of the main centres for art and history in the whole of the country.
If you look at our map, you'll see some of the main attractions there.
Most visitors start from Crawley Road, at the bottom of the map.
Reynolds House 出现，第一题开始
The Reynolds House is one of the oldest houses in the city, and is open to the public.
位于 Crawley Road 的北侧，靠近通向 public garden 的小路
It's on the north side of Crawley Road, next to the footpath that leads to the public gardens.
The area's particularly interesting for its unusual sculptures.
Thumb 出现，第二题开始
The Thumb' is just what its name suggests, but it's about 10 metres high.
在 Hill Road 上，bank 的对面（across the road 从… 一边到另一边，在… 对面 ）
You'll see it on Hill Road, across the road from the Bank.
Museum 出现，第三题开始
The Museum's got a particularly fine collection of New Zealand landscapes.
在 City road 上
It's on the east side of the Sheepmarket, on City Road.
在 public gardens 的另一侧，正对着 Hill road 的交叉路口（junction）
It's on the other side of the road from the public gardens, immediately facing the junction with Hill Road.
Contemporary Art Gallery 出现，第四题开始，在一条通往 Station Square 的小路上，离 public gardens 不远
The Contemporary Art Gallery is on a little road that leads off Station Square, not far from the public gardens.
这条小路的尽头就是这个 Contemporary Art gallery
The road ends at the gallery - it doesn't go anywhere else.
That's open every day except Mondays.
Warner Gallery 出现，第五题开始
The Warner Gallery specialises in 19th-century art.
在 City Road 上，在它与 Crawley Road 的交叉口的附近，在 public gardens 的同一侧
It's on City Road, near the junction with Crawley Road, on the same side of the road as the public gardens.
It's open on weekdays from 9 to 5, and entry is free.
Nucleus 出现，第六题开始
Finally, if you're interested in purchasing high quality artwork, the place to go is Nucleus.
从 Crawley Road 向上穿过 Station Square，然后沿着 Hill Road 往东走，直到一个小蜿蜒崎岖的小路
You need to go from Crawley Road up through Station Square and east along Hill Road until you get to a small winding road turning off.
就在你的右边，如果你一直走到了 City Road，你就走过了
Go up there and it's on your right - if you get to City Road you've gone too far.

# 剑 11 Test 1 Section 2


开始介绍布局
Now let me give you some idea of the layout of the farm.
The building where you bought your tickets is the New Barn, immediately to your right, and we're now at the beginning of the main path to the farmland — and of course the car park is on your left.
Scarecrow 在停车场的角落里，挨着 main path
The scarecrow you can see in the car park in the corner, beside the main path, is a traditional figure for keeping the birds away from crops, but our scarecrow is a permanent sculpture.
It's taller than a human being, so you can see it from quite a distance.
往前看，可以看到一个 maze
If you look ahead of you, you'll see a maze.
在 new barn 的对面，分岔路的右边，符合条件的只有 G 了
It's opposite the New Barn, beside the side path that branches off to the right just over there.
The maze is made out of hedges which are too tall for young children to see over them, but it's quite small, so you can't get lost in it!
非常显眼的 Fish Pool 和桥，要过河了？
Now, can you see the bridge crossing the fish pool further up the main path?
说到 cafe，我们现在桥的南边 main path 上，朝着桥走，在到之前向右拐，难道是 D 吗
If you want to go to the café, go towards the bridge and turn right just before it.
沿着分岔路一直走，餐厅就在你们看到的第一个拐弯的地方
Walk along the side path and the café's on the first bend you come to.
The building was originally the schoolhouse, and it's well over a hundred years old.
As you may know, we run skills workshops here, where you can learn traditional crafts like woodwork and basket-making.
要开始说 Black Barn 了
You can see examples of the work, and talk to someone about the courses, in the Black Barn.
从 New Barn 沿着 side path 向右走，在第一个拐弯的地方就能找到 Black Barn
If you take the side path to the right, here, just by the New Barn, you'll come to the Black Barn just where the path first bends.
说到 picnick
Now I mustn't forget to tell you about picnicking, as I can see some of you have brought your lunch with you.
You can picnic in the field, though do clear up behind you, of course.
farmyard 的附近，过了桥之后，右边就是 covered picnic area
Or if you'd prefer a covered picnic area, there's one near the farmyard: just after you cross the bridge, there's a covered picnic spot on the right.
说到 Fiddy House
And the last thing to mention is Fiddy House itself.
沿着 farm yard 左边的 footpath，穿过田野
From here you can cross the bridge then walk along the footpath through the field to the left of the farmyard.
就能到达 Fiddy House
That goes to the house, and it'll give you a lovely view of it.
It's certainly worth a few photographs, but as it's a private home, I'm afraid you can't go inside.
Right. Well, if you're all ready, we'll set off on our tour of the farm.

# 剑 11 Test 4 Section 2


开始
Most of the museum facilities are downstairs, in the basement, so you go down the stairs here.
When you reach the bottom of the stairs, you'll find yourself in a sitting area,with comfortable chairs and sofas where you can have a rest before continuing your exploration of the museum.
说到 restaurant
We have a very good restaurant, which serves excellent food all day, in a relaxing atmosphere.
向右拐入一个走廊
To reach it, when you get to the bottom of the stairs, go straight ahead to the far side of the sitting area, then turn right into the corridor.
你就能看到餐厅正对着你，所以是 F 区
You'll see the door of the restaurant facing you.
说到 cafe
If you just want a snack, or if you'd like to eat somewhere with facilities for children, we also have a café.
还是沿着刚才的走廊然后 cafe 在你的右边，所以是 H 区
When you reach the bottom of the stairs, you'll need to go straight ahead, turn right into the corridor, and the café is immediately on the right.
说到 baby-changing facilities，穿过 sitting area 一直沿着走廊走，就在你的左侧，因为是一直走所以是 C 区
And talking about children, there are baby-changing facilities downstairs: cross the sitting area, continue straight ahead along the corridor on the left, and you and your baby will find the facilities on the left-hand side.
说到 cloakroom，就在 sitting area 的左手边
The cloakroom, where you should leave coats, umbrellas and any large bags, is on the left hand side of the sitting area.
在走入走廊前的最后一扇门，很明显是 B 区
It's through the last door before you come to the corridor.
There are toilets on every floor, but in the basement they're the first rooms on the left when you get down there.
OK, now if you've got anything to leave in the cloakroom, please do that now, and then we'll start our tour.

# 剑 11 Test 2 Section 2


Standing here in the foyer, you're probably wondering why the box office isn't here, where the public would expect to find it.
就在进来之间，有两个入口，隔壁门，所以是 box office 位于 G 区
Well, you might have noticed it on your way in - although it's part of this building, it's next door, with a separate entrance from the road.
说到 theatre manager's office，穿过 foyer，通过 double doors，向右，在走廊的尽头，向左，所以是 D 区
For the theatre manager's office, you go across the foyer and through the double doors, turn right, and it's the room at the end of the corridor, with the door on the left.
说到 lighting box
The lighting box is where the computerised stage lighting is operated, and it's at the back of the building.
通过 double doors，向左，再向右，再向右
When you're through the double doors, turn left, turn right at the water cooler, and right again at the end.
走廊里的第二间，所以是 B
It's the second room along that corridor.
The lighting box has a window into the auditorium, which of course is below us.
说到 artistic director's office，通过 double doors，向右右手边第一间，所以是 F
The artistic director's office is through the double doors, turn right, and it's the first room you come to on the right-hand side.
And finally, for the moment, the room where I'll take you next - the relaxation room.
So if you'd like to come with me ...
