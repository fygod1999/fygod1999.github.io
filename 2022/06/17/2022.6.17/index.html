# 2022.6.17 口语课堂练习 & 作业

Describe a website on the Internet that you use regularly.

You should say:

  • what sort of website it is
  • what you use it for
  • what you like and dislike about it

and explain why you use it regularly.

what:leetcode
what:practice and consolidate knowledge
how:improve my way of thinking and algorithm skills

The website i want to describe is Leetcode and it's a platform for coders to practise algorithm skills in China and has already spread to the developers all over the world.It has a very simple and elegant user interface design,you would never lose your way while using the Leetcode.Each category has obvious meanings and contains some questions,labeled by some topics.Hence whatever type of topic you want to practise,just make a finding.If you are confused about a problem after you trying hard,you can get the key wiritten by others for help,what's more,it's a good way for you to improve your skill by writing your own thought of solution.I was told by my roomates about this platform when i was a freshman,and i have participated in it since that time.With my strong will,my skill has been improved a lot.
I have been using Leetcode for nearly three years now.For me,its advantages are not limited to the guidebooks and the variety of problems,but it also has a forum for all the coders on the platform by sharing and accessing some resources even the job hunting information.
But there are also things I don't like about it as it sometimes hits my confidence round by round which disturb my schedule if i don't have a strong will.

Describe an electronic device you use often.

You should say:

  • how long you have had it
  • how often you have used it
  • what you have used it for

and explain why you use it so often.

what:laptop
what:write essay,code,play 3A works
how:convenience,adaptability,portability,directly

The electronic device i want to describe is my laptop and it has accompanied me for three years,though it has some trouble with its component parts,it can solve problems very fast.Three year ago,i bought this laptop from a DELL exclusive store.As my first laptop,it seems meet all my needs,with appearance of thin notebook and the vigorous core.Like many other laptops,it didn't have any special function,but the reason why i choose it is because its high cost-performance ratio.However,I can't imagine the advantage thought by myself would be the disadvantage one day in the future.After two years' using,the battery got some problem because the cpu power consumption is too high.Hence i have to reserve a new battery.After i receiving the new battery,the other mistake occured to me that it's impossible for me to disassemble the laptop without some tools.So i send both the laptop and the new battery to one of my friends who is proficient at repairing.Finally,he helped me solve the problem.
I have been using it for nearly three years now.For me,its advantages include its convenience,adaptability and portability with silver appearance.
But there are also some disadvantages about it that its high cpu consumption
don't match its battery load and the gap between the hinge turn bigger as you use it more.

# 2022.6.14 口语作业

Describe an English lesson that you really enjoyed.

You should say:

  • where and when it took place
  • who the teacher was
  • what you did in the lesson

and explain why you enjoyed it so much.

Well the english lesson i'm gonna talk about is the one i had in my junior year in university,which have been half of a year ago by now.
It wasn't the first time that given by my english teacher Miss Fen in the university,during the whole freshman year and sophomore year,she gave us the collge student's english.And before the ending of the sophomore year,i found that she might be the only one teacher who accompanied with us and witnessed our growth in university,she was always very kind to us,but it might be the last few lessons she can taught us.A few of my friends have the same feeling with me,upset and hesitation about future life in university.However,things made a change afterwards,according to the decision of our college meeting,we would have an academic English on the first semester of our Junior year,and the teacher is Miss Fen,i remember i was so excited to heard of this at that time.
On the first lesson of academic english,all of my classmates attended and Miss Fen was also very glad to see us again on the class and then she gave us the main structure of academic english using curcumstances like a disseartation and an interview and what should be acknowledged by us.Actually,all of the lessons she gave us are enjoyable,this feeling not only comes from my admiration to her dedication and equality to any of her students,but also comes from the precious content.
