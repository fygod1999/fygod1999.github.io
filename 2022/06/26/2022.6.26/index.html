# 听力（填空）

# 6-2-2

They leave Trebirch every half hour on weekdays and every hour at weekends.It takes about two hours, a bit longer on Sundays.

虽然做对了，但刚才做的时候有点疑惑，题目说 each day during the week，我当时有点纳闷，到底是 each day during the weekdays，还是整个一周，因为音频后面又说了 Sunday 和 weekends 耗时上可能存在的差异，因此琢磨着难不成要我算一个平均数？因为来不及计算，果断填了听到的第一个时间 half hour，没想到对了，看来 each day during the week 指的就是周一到周五，不含周末。

You've got a choice of first and second class and there's a buffet car - though refreshments are included in the cost of a first class ticket.

后面读的有点快，而且 though 基本没听出来，很模糊，也不知道 refreshment（点心）到底可不可数，不过又听了一遍，发现确实 refreshment 后面发的是 “つ”，而非 “ｔ”，平时练习的时候真的得多留心一些名词的结尾！

The cheapest tickets are called Advance and you have to buy them at least six days ahead.

这个明摆是 Advance，不改错的。

Only a certain number are available and you have to make seat reservations for these.

真没注意到，还得练！reservations 也是可数名词
