# 流程图写作

  • 常用语法：一般现在时 + 被动语态
  • 使用图中所给出的信息
  • 注意流程始末
  • 判断生词词性和用法
  • 使用顺序词，逐步描述
  1. 单一流程：开头段（标题改写）- 设备组成 - 工作流程 - 结尾（再次改写）
  2. 混合流程：开头段（标题改写）- 图一流程 - 图二流程 - 结尾段（对比）

顺序词

  • 首先：first，the process begins when...，in the first stage of the process
  • 接连发生：as a result，when the wave goes down，as a consequence，following this，in the next stage，next，then
  • 同时发生：as，as the same time，meanwhile
  • 最后：lastly，finally

# 被动语态

  • 使用原因：主语不必要，或者主语不明确
  • 使用规则：动词接收者 + be + 动词过去分词

# Unit 5 小作文（流程图）

The diagram below show the design for a turbine and its location.

Summarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features,and make comparisons where relevant.

The diagrams illustrate the main components of the wind turbine with how each part works and makes a comparison of three setting locations with their features respectively.
A wind turbine consists of a generator,a steel tower and a computer room.First it comes to the generator,wind drives the three wooden or fibreglass blades and makes them rotated round by round.On the joint of the three blades,there is a wind sensor,it transfers information to the computer room which can control the speed and direction of blades by adjusting their direction and angle.The generator is settled on a straight steel tower,it can generate up to 1.5 megawatts.
According to the diagram,there are three possible locations for a turbine.First,the largest wind turbine can be built on the top of hill with strongest wind and the output can reach to the peak.By contrast,the family used turbine looks smaller but it can be placed near a house and outputs 100 kilowatts.Another locating option for a turbine is in the sea without any occupation of land here and keeps the advantage of strong wind.
To sum up,the strength of wind is a main factor for a wind turbine and its output depends on the intensity of the wind there.
