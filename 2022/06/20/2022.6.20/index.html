以下是本季度的 part2&3 人物类话题

本次课老师对于名人、普通人各举了一个例子

开头：Well,I'm going to talk about Chris Martin,who is a rock singer from England.

个人信息（职业 / 名声）：You may not heard of this person before,but you must know about the band Coldplay.He is the leader singer of the band and its songs Yellow,The Scientist are popular among us the people like me students and some young people.

如何了解（作品）：I've got to know about him because once,a friend of mine played the music singing app We Sing and when i clicked one of his version covered song,i was deeply touched by the rythem and the its melody,then i searched it on the Internet,it's a song called Yellow performed by Coldplay,after that i always listen to their songs and i got to that Chris Martin,a very handsome English man,graduated from UCL,is the leader of the band.

银幕上（音乐才华，获得奖项）：Chris Martin doesn't act like a rock musician with tattoos to the public.He is more like a little boy full of energy and can use something comes from nature to express his love.Just like he sings in yellow "the stars shine for you" ,the lyric iteself,his unique gentle voice and the eclectic style makes me immersed in his gorgeous performance.In the song Everglow he wrote to his former wife,Martin still used his soft voice to tell people a sad story all the time.He's a Pisces just like me,so i really understand his mood when he sing this song.

人物品质（个人理解）：Martin came from UCL, where he met several young people with the same ideals and dreams as him. Later, they formed Coldplay.Chris tells his dream, love and attitude towards life with his unique voice again and again. He is what he looks like,a gentlemen who affectionating towards love,retaining the spirit of childhood.


Well,I would like to talk about one of my friends,he is nearly the second person i got to know in the university and we are in the same class.I remember that day,i met him before the first class meeting which was hold for a class committee election.At that time,I was a little bit nervous and preparing for my speech for the election on the dormitory balcony while he was talking on the phone,after the phone call,he greeted with me and we talked about the election.I found he is a very kind and clever person to help me with the speech word,the suitable performance on the election and analysis the process of an election for me.Later on,both of us got the job we want smoothly.
During the work time,he is talented with methodology,when some problems in study occurs to me,he can cope with them most of the time.Maybe he doesn't know the final answer,but he can find the solution.As you know,he does more than he talks,so when he finds the solution of one step,he keeps doing research on the deeper layers.He is also gifted in playing league of legends.He is qualified with the skills and good mentality.
He always keeps optimic and shows his kindness to his frends,he is a talkative guy and when i was upset,he always encourages me.Though he is younger than me,he has rich life experience.It's nice to have such a intellectual friend like him.
