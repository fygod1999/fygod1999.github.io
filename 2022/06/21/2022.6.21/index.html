# 听力同义替换句（剑 10）

refurbished fitness suite & ...improved...gym
Our recently refurbished fitness suite has all the latest exercise equipment including ten new running machines.

take through & explain
The trainer will then take you through the safety rules for using the equipment in the fitness suite.

preferences...at peak times & priority...peak hours
Premier membership is for professional people whose work commitments make it difficult for them to use the club during the day and so LP gives booking preferences to Premier members at peak times.

come up with & develop
Well,it's an international design competition and we have to come up with a new design for a typical domestic kitchen appliance.

looks different & appealing
I think some people will be prepared to pay a little extra for something that looks different.

turn & switch it on
Instead of pushing a button you turn the stone.

revert & change
That's where the liquid is depressurised and so it reverts to a gas.
