# P16 ex.10 小作文


The bar chart shows the difficulties shared by people who move to another country and how the problems vary according to people's ages.
Overall,the middle aged people have the highest percentage of all the problems.About 37 percent of them have healthcare problem,more than the older people with the percentage of 36,and fewer younger people(32 percent)take this problem into consideration.
However,the younger ones need to consider more of the financial problems than the elders,there are 34 percent of the young people experience this problem while only 28 percent of the older ones have this problem.In comparison,people aged from 35-54 pay more attention to this problem than the others,at 37 percent.
The problem providing their children with education experienced by the least number of people at all ages,with about 6 percent from 18-34,18 percent from 35-54 and only 2 percent over 55.
In summary,middle aged people face the most problems,which come from work,family and health.Young people need to think more about the finance than the older ones while the older ones need to think more about the health than the young people.
