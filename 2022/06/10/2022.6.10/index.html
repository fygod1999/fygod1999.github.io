LOH 主旨题

特点

  • 文章前出现
  • 考察主旨
  • 有例题
  • 选项 > 题目

方法

  • 找转折，扫读段落，寻找诸如 but，however，while 一类的转折词，如果一个句子出现了转折，那么其作用多半是用来承上启下的，所以关注转折后的内容即可。
  • 找例子，扫读段落中的 example，段落中出现的例子是用来证明观点的，观点和例子的顺序有两种：先说观点，后面加上例子来论述观点；先讲事例，最后总结观点。所以，如果在文章中看见举例，那么这一段的主旨往往就在例子的前一句或者后一句中。
  • 首二末一：关注首两句和最后一句，雅思阅读的文章大多是 “（总）分（总）” 结构，因此首末处往往会是这一段的主旨，做题时可以按照顺序先读第一句，如果第一句不是主旨而是过渡句，那么第二句往往才是该段的主旨，如果还不是，看最后一句。

多选题

特点

  • 选择乱序
  • 题目包含明确的提问对象
  • 找到问题中的关键词，定位文章，每一题基本都是在同一段中出现？再判断每个选项会容易一些
  • 干扰项基本都是原词（看到原词一定得谨慎，基本是坑），还是得关注同义替换
  • 每一题的出题是顺序的？要注意句子层面上的逻辑关系。

Could you describe the place where you live?

The place where I live is a small city called Ma'anshan, located in the southeast of Anhui Province, near the Yangtze River. Because I stayed there before going to university, I know a lot about it, the city can date back to 1950s, because we have rich iron ore resources here and there are some riverside Iron factories remained by the Japanese during world war two, so in that era of vigorous development of heavy industry, the city came into being. Although there are no high peaks or spectacular lakes, there are some great historical and cultural landscapes, such as Li Bai's tomb and Caishiji on the edge of cliff near the Yangtze River. Due to being located between the two big cities of Nanjing and Wuhu, our economy has developed rapidly in recent years, and people live happily.

How well do you know your neighbours?

Though my neighbour have been moved,the grandfather our of neighbour is very kind to us and help us a lot,so I can’t forget that period of time.I remember that time I were in high school.Once I forget to bring my key back home after school.I didn’t have smart phone at that time and my parents often backs home so early.So I have no place to go to do my homework.When I were anxious,knocking the door,my neighbour open the door and let me in,bring me food and a place to do the homework.So I am grateful to him.

Do you like travel by car?

I like travel by car and that is the reason why I don’t like travel by train or plane.During the whole trip,I can drive to anywhere I want to go as long as bring enough money and gas,this process is like DIY,I can set my own travel route and enjoy the scenery along the way.But I think it also has some disadvantages of travelling by car is that we should make a budget before the journey or we will cost more money than travel by train or plain and travel by car is not very convenient for a long distance travel.
