The charts below show the results of a survey on happiness ratings for married and unmarried people in the Us, and the effect of children on the overall ratings of married couples.
Summarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant.


The bar charts illustrate whether the Americans enjoy themselves or not because of marriage across age groups and show the distrubition of happiness among three types of couples which according to their children's condition.

In terms of the marriage, people satisfied with their life who have been married take up the percentage of 40 aged from 50-64. Apart from this age group of people, married people nearly have the same proportion of happiness, at about 44 percent respectively, with only 1 percent of difference between 18-29 and the other two groups.The lowest percentage of people think happy of their life in ages ranging from 18 to 64 who haven't been married, at slightly over 20 percent.By contrast,more singles with older age have the satisfaction of their life without marriage.

When it comes to the children, 44 percent of couples feel happy with children younger than 18, followed by the family without children at 43 percent, while there is only 41 percent of couples with children age 18 years or older are happy.

Overall, the level of happiness in the United States is less than 50%. However, married people tend to be happier than unmarried people, whether they have children or not.（to tell you the truth，一开始真没想到如何概括全篇）
