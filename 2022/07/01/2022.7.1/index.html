Besides, you can also buy things that are big or heavy, like a fridge or dining table because shipping has become quite efficient and cost-effective.

# Company advertising

How important do you think it is for companies to advertise their products?

You know，Advertising is a good way to introduce products of manufacturers and attract consumers to buy these products. By that I mean advertisements can guarantee a satisfactory sale.

In our country, you can see that the ads displayed in the internet are becoming increasingly frequent while those on newspaper are generally reduced. So in the future，the ads on internet will play a dominant role in advertising.

How do advertisements influence people to buy things?

Some of them would have great influences on people before buying, people buys the product because of their needs, and ads provide customers with the details and clear lists to choose from. It's the first step for them to have information about the products and its company.I would say the most creative and stimulating one leave the deepest impression on people. You know，nowadays, there are excessively numerous ads, folks have already been fed up with the boring ads

How effective is advertising as a way of persuading people to buy things?

Obviously，it does. It becomes a trend for every well-known brand to make advertisements. Coz it can enhance and boost the power and reputation of the brand effectively.When the products they introduce are really attractive and I do need this goods. In other word, if there are several ads showing the same thing I will choose the better performance one.
