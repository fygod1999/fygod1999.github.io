# 2022.6.29 写作

The graph below and the chart on the next page show the answers people gave about the extent to which they are satisfied with their lives and what they think would make them happiest.

The bar chart illustrates the factors which needed by people the most from different age groups and the percentage of each factor.

In terms of health, the highest percentage of people over 65 regarded it as the most enjoyable thing,at around 45 percent whereas the quantity between 15 to 24 was the least,at about 12 percent compared to the people aged around 30 who showed more interested with health at 20 percent.

In the contrary, this part of people considered money as the most precious thing, with the percentage of nearly 55, followed by people aged from 25 to 34,at slightly over 50 percent.People between 45 to 54 treated health as important as money basically, but money is more attractive, about 4% more than the attraction of health.

Overall, as people grow older, increasingly amount of them preferred health to be the happiest thing while there was a downward trend of money's temptation for most of people.

# 剑 5 Test 2 Section 2

13.But in order to continue operating we need to have a constant supply of bikes which we send out every six months.

need to have a constant supply of bikes = getting enough bicycles to send regularly

Also, you could help by contacting the voluntary staff at our offices, they'll be able to suggest activities you could organise to bring in funds for us.

hold an event to raise money = activities

We hope that by talking on radio programmes like this, we will be able to raise public awareness, which will lead to government organisations also giving us regular financial support, something that we really need.

identify areas that need bikes = something that we really need

It actually opened as a retail chain selling camping equipment, and then twenty years ago, it bought a small number of campsites in the UK, and began offering camping holidays.

Twenty years ago, PS Camping has been organising holidays.

If you haven't organised an annual insurance policy of your own you'll need to take out the low-cost cover we offer and we require that you arrange this when you make your holiday reservation.
