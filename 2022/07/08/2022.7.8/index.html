# 听力

# 剑 7 Test 2 Section 4

35.Studies showed that apes consistently using the same hand fished out 30% more ants than those varying between the two.

apes which always use the same hand to get food are most successful == same hand fished out 30% more

40.The predominant reason for this is because it aids balance which is of course absolutely central to performance in this sport.

aids balance 帮助平衡性

# 剑 7 Test 3 Section 4

31.You wake up in the middle of the night in a strange hotel miles away from home, disoriented most probably from jet lag, when even the most expensive surroundings can seem empty and dispiriting.

什么意思？

你半夜在离家数英里外的一家陌生酒店醒来，很可能是因为时差而迷失了方向，即使是最昂贵的环境也可能显得空虚和沮丧。

dispiriting == depressed

33.The company's research covered a whole range of different hotel types, both independent hotels and those which are part of large chains.

a whole range of == a wide variety of

# 剑 13 Test 4 Section 3

26.Those findings weren't that conclusive and it was quite a small-scale study.

did not produce clear results == weren't that conclusive

27.ALICE: I think supermarkets like the idea of having a colour-coded system - red, orange or green - for levels of fat, sugar and salt in a product.

colour-coded system 即 traffic-light system，Alice 认为超市是喜欢这个系统的

30.They should have focused on people with low literacy levels because these labels are designed to be accessible to them.

注意这里用的是 should，虚拟语气，事实上并没有，所以 A 项不选

30.ALICE: No. But if they hadn't interviewed participants face-to-face, they could have used a much bigger sample size.

if they hadn't interviewed participants face-to-face，说明 face to face 采访是发生过的，所以 E 项正确
